About Us

IRIS scans was started in 2008 to bring high end ultrasound technology solutions to patients outside the frame work of corporate hospitals. Emphasis on technology & expertise has made it perhaps the most popular premium segment ultrasound centre in the twin cities.

The centre works as a Radiology Group Practice much like group practices of physicians in US & Europe. Its’ owned by radiologists and is a great place for radiologists to work and patients get the personal touch that makes all the difference. It is managed by a team that follows patient service & and Quality norms & monitors work flow & user satisfaction levels in accordance with applicable accredition standards. ‘Ampath’ of the top 10 trusted pathology brands in the world is now a part of the IRIS team. Cancer detection staging and grading , chromosomal and DNA studies are now available.

IRIS undertakes technology trainings for Doctors and supportive skills trainings for paramedical staff regularly.

Seminars & group activities often handled with a picnic or a lunch over a Sunday are conducted as per event calendar to raise awareness & understanding of technology related issues among the hospital & Paramedical communities.
